📋 Snack Cost Sharing Policy

Please read before placing your order

Snack costs are shared equally

The total price of all snacks ordered during a break session will be divided equally among everyone who places a beverage order (tea, coffee, or any other drink).

Participation implies contribution

By placing a beverage order through Sip n Snack, you automatically consent to your proportional share of snack costs for that session, regardless of whether you personally consumed any snack.

No individual snack attribution

Snacks are considered a communal item. The app does not track which individual consumed which snack, and no adjustments will be made based on personal snack consumption.

No post-session disputes

Once a session is closed and costs are settled, the charges are final. You waive all rights to raise billing disputes, demand refunds, or contest the snack cost split after settlement.

Transparency of charges

Your session summary will display the total snack cost, the number of participants, and your individual share before any deduction is made.
